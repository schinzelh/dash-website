Exodus, in conjunction with the Dash core development team, is pleased to announce Dash support inside the latest Exodus early preview available today.

Exodus brings a friendly experience to decentralized tokens like Bitcoin, Dash, Ethereum and more. Adding Dash support furthers the Exodus foundation of a decentralized multi­asset experience to secure, exchange and manage wealth inside one beautiful application.

Exodus keeps all keys and transaction data locally for your eyes only ­ no accounts, no servers, no data sharing. In addition to a focus on privacy Exodus supports the following:

  • ●  1­click exchanges between Dash and other assets. Exodus is the first desktop multi­asset wallet with ShapeShift built in.
  • ●  No more messy copy/paste exchanges between wallets. Exodus is the only way to exchange Ethereum for Dash inside one application.
  • ●  Exodus wraps solid engineering inside beautiful design. From pixel­perfect icons to subtle sounds, Exodus is a multi­asset experience that "just works for normal people".
  • ●  Track the value of your multi­asset portfolio, in real time, as market conditions change. Quote from Evan Duffield, Dash lead developer:“Exodus is a wonderfully simple, easy to use and beautiful cryptocurrency wallet. It's exactly what’s needed in the space right now and we’re really happy to be a part of it.”
