Here at Bitwala we continuously strive to push the boundaries and provide our customers with the best experience of using Bitcoins for their daily finances. With this in mind, we are proud to announce our partnership with our friends at ShapeShift.io, the leading online exchange for blockchain assets.

With ShapeShift.io onboard the Bitwala boat, we now accept all major cryptocurrencies!

We see Bitwala as a bridge between traditional and modern banking that is easy, secure and accessible to anyone who would like to use Bitcoin or Altcoins in their daily finances.
