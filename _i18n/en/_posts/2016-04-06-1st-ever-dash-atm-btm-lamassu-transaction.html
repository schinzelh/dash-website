As you might know , Dash is working on Lamassu ATM's integration.
This project (as all) is Funded by DGBB
Announcement:
https://dashtalk.org/threads/january-2016-budget-proposal.7392/
Voting:
https://www.dashwhale.org/p/lamassu-integration

We are very please to present the 1st ever Dash Lamassu ATM Transaction !
Tx to Ira Miller and his Team, the software is still in testing , but will be released to the public/ BTM Operators very soon. The options then are:
Dash only
Dash / BTC parallel on 1 Machine.
When complete and released, Dash will be one of the 1st Ever Altcoins (next to Eth) to be available on Bitcoin ATM's
