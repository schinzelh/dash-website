Secure, Shared

Dash Wallets

Dash Copay is a true wallet, not an account service. You keep personal control of the keys to your funds at all times. Or, like a joint-checking account, you can choose to share control with multiple signatures. To send money, the group approves each transaction.

Manage shared funds with friends and coworkers. Share a wallet with your child and introduce them to Dash. Dash Copay makes sharing a wallet simple and secure.

Instant, User-Friendly

Payments

Enjoy full payment confirmation in under two seconds with Dash Copay. Built on InstantSend technology unique to the Dash network, you will never have to wait for slow block confirmations again.

User-friendly address books and annotated transaction history makes keeping track of your finances a breeze.

Open Source

Transparent Security

Dash is committed to open source technology, and Dash Copay is no exception. Both the wallet and server are built using industry-standard best practices, and source code is available to be reviewed, improved, and developed by the community.

A simple passphrase is all that is necessary to backup your wallet and restore full access to your funds if anything happens to your device.
